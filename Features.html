What can safelight do?

Click on any tab to learn more.

Warn you when you are about to hit a yellow light

SafeLight is capable of calculating weather or not it is safe to continue through a yellow light or weather it would be preferable to stop. This warning would help reduce drivers' anxiety and prevent poor decision making that could cause a driver to run a red light

Countdown until the next green light

Safelight, being connected to nearby traffic lights, is able to display the time in seconds until your next green light. This makes intersections a more predictable experience

Mutes audio when an emergency vehicle is approching

SafeLight can detect nearby emergency vehicles. SafeLight mutes all car audio, when an emergy vehicle is in range. SafeLight could then be an important tool for first responders.
